This is me

For the past three years I have been pursuing a career in yacht racing, with an ultimate ambition of competing in and winning the Volvo Ocean Race. To me, offshore yacht racing is the ultimate combination of competition, challenge, and adventure.

Next to sailing I’m also a full stack web developer. I love solving challenging problems and in my free time I enjoy designing and building some web applications I think are pretty cool. In the past I have worked as a business analyst developing cloud banking software, and I was part of the team that migrated the first bank in the world completely into the cloud.

Feel free to check out some more information about me in the sections below and if you're interested in working together, please get in touch!
